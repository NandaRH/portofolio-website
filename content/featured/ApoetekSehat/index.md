A comprehensive web-based pharmacy inventory management system designed to streamline operations for small pharmacies. Features include real-time stock tracking with expiration monitoring, multi-role access control (Admin, Owner, Staff), and a centralized dashboard for visualizing transaction history and critical business metrics.
