A full-stack e-commerce platform engineered for a specialty coffee brand. The application digitizes the entire retail workflow, featuring:
**Robust Admin Dashboard:** Comprehensive content management system (CMS) for real-time inventory tracking, order processing, and sales reporting.
**Secure Transactions:** Implemented secure JWT-based authentication and role-based access control (RBAC) to protect sensitive user and admin data.
**Advanced Logistics:** Integrated coordinate-based shipping calculation logic to provide accurate delivery estimates for customers.
**Cloud Asset Management:** Utilized Cloudinary for optimized media storage and delivery of high-quality product assets.
